About Craftsvilla.

Craftsvilla brings your creative vision to life with engaging workshops led by our passionate team — Raj, Sofia, and Elena. Together, they help you create more than crafts — they help you create memories that last.

Meet our talented artist-teachers

Raj Kapoor – Mosaic Art

Master the Art of Mosaics with Raj’s expert guidance in creating vibrant, stone-filled masterpieces.

Sofia Müller – Watercolor Painting

Immerse yourself in peaceful, fluid creativity as Sofia teaches the delicate art of watercolor painting.

Elena Rossi – Canvas Painting

Explore your boldest artistic side with Elena’s dynamic canvas painting workshops that encourage creativity and self-expression.
